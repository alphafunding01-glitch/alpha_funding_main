# Changelog - Alpha Funding Platform

## [2026-01-05]
### Added
- **Deep Intelligence API**: Integrated Profile, Officers, Charges, PSC, and Insolvency parallel fetching.
- **Zero-Friction Pre-fill**: Added "invisible" address (Line 1, City, Postcode) and Industry fields that auto-populate upon company search.
- **Director Selection Dropdown**: Implemented dynamic choice for multi-director companies in Apply Now and Check Eligibility.
- **Product Recommendation**: Generative UI now recommends specific loan products based on company SIC codes and industry data.
- **Internal Risk Capture**: Form submissions now include hidden intelligence (Charges/Insolvency) for lead management.

### Changed
- Moved "Company Name" to the primary position (top) in all onboarding forms.
- **SIC to Industry Translation**: AI now converts SIC codes to readable industry names for auto-pre-fill.
- **Intelligence Badges**: Added visual indicators for Insolvency, Major Owners (PSC), and Active Charges.
- **Dark Mode Support**: Generative UI now adjusts text colors for dark-themed layouts (Check Eligibility).
- **Enriched AI Prompts**: Product recommendations are now bolded and more specific to the industry footprint.
